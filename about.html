Hi there! I'm Christina, a multi-diciplinary designer based in Lenapehoking—so called Brooklyn, NY. Most recently I was the Design Lead for One More Multiverse, a role playing game platform. In the past I’ve focused on building the learning platform for Codecademy, been a teacher and research resident at NYU ITP, a product designer at an education start up, and an art director for children's books and graphic novels. Here's my most up to date resume but you can find the long, winding version of the professional story here.

Creativity is a practice and I exercise as much as I can in my free time. I'm interested in studying how we can create a healthy social fabric on the web. Other times I make small, playful experiences for myself and others. My favorite projects have almost always made in conversation with other people.
