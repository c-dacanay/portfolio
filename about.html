Hi there. I'm Christina, a designer and artist living in Brooklyn.

Currently I'm the Design Lead for One More Multiverse, a role playing game platform.

I'm also an adjunct professor for NYU's ITP program, where I went to graduate school, and a freelance product and visual designer.

In the past I’ve been a fellow at NYU, a product designer at an education start up, an art director in children’s book publishing, and an educator. You can find the long, winding version of the professional story here.

Sometimes in my personal work I try to examine how we can create a healthy social fabric on the web. Other times I make small, playful experiences for myself and others.

Most of my favorite things have been made with other people. Let's make something together!
