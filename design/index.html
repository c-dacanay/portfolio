Hi! I'm Christina. I'm a multi-disciplinary designer and illustrator based in Lenapehoking, so called Brooklyn.

I'm currently the Design Lead at One More Multiverse where we help bring TTRPGs to life. Before that I worked as a Product Designer at Codecademy where I focused on creating a streamlined and engaging platform for learners. I used to art direct children's books and graphic novels as a Senior Designer for Penguin Random House and Macmillan. I've also done a variety of freelance visual, product, and UX design.

I've ran design sprints during blue sky phases, conducted research to gather requirements, and focused on fully zoomed-in UI polish. I genuinely love every part of the process so long as it contributes to a shared goal with a good team. Plus, learning is always fun. My resume has more skills and specialities.

Creativity is a practice and I exercise as much as I can outside of work. I've got another website for the artsy stuff, if you like. My favorite projects have always made in collaboration with, or alongside, other people.
