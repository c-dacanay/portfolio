Hi there! I'm Christina.

Currently I'm the Design Lead for One More Multiverse, a role playing game platform.

In the past I’ve been a teacher and research resident at NYU ITP, a product designer at an education start up, and an art director in children’s book publishing. You can find the long, winding version of the professional story here.

Sometimes in my personal work I'm interested in studying how we can create a healthy social fabric on the web. Other times I make small, playful experiences for myself and others.

Most of my favorite things have been made with other people. ☜ Send me an email to say hi!
