In a few words

Experimental games start-up creating a visual platform for tabletop games

My role
  • Facilitating ideation workshops
  • UX/UI
  • User research
  • Product development

One More Multiverse is a wholly unique platform for telling cinematic stories through tabletop games. I began as the sole UX designer and was later promoted to design lead, where I lead every part of the design process from brainstorming, user research, to product design and UI development.
